## 业务简介
Constellation Brands, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, produces, imports, markets, and sells beer, wine, and spirits in the United States, Canada, Mexico, New Zealand, and Italy. The company offers beer under the Corona Extra, Corona Familiar, Corona Hard Seltzer, Corona Light, Corona Non-Alcohol, Modelo Especial, Modelo Negra, Modelo Chelada, Pacifico, and Victoria brands; and wine and spirits under the Black Box, Casa Noble, Charles Shaw, Clos du Bois, Cooper & Thief, Kim Crawford, Meiomi, Mount Veeder, Ruffino, The Prisoner, and Woodbridge brands.
